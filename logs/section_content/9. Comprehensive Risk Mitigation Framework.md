## Strategic Risk Management

Siemens views risk management not merely as a compliance requirement but as a fundamental component of its overall strategic framework. This perspective indicates a proactive approach to identifying, assessing, and mitigating risks that could impact the company's strategic objectives, financial performance, and long-term sustainability. Integrating risk management into strategic planning enables Siemens to make more informed decisions, allocate resources effectively, and enhance its resilience to unforeseen challenges.
