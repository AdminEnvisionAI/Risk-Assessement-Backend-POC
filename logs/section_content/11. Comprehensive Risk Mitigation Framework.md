## Cybersecurity Risks

Siemens Energy recognizes cybersecurity as a critical risk, emphasizing the need to protect valuable assets, critical infrastructure, and sensitive data/intellectual property. Failure to adequately address cybersecurity risks could lead to operational disruptions, data breaches, intellectual property theft, and reputational damage. The company views risk management not merely as a compliance issue but as an integral part of their overall strategy, suggesting a proactive approach to risk management.
